Why Mental Health is important

Mental health is crucial because it affects every aspect of our lives, including how we think, feel, and act. When our mental health is well, we are better able to handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Poor mental health can lead to emotional and physical problems, impacting our relationships, work, and overall well-being.
